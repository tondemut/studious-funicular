    About Us

History

With over 30 years of proven performance and results, we pride ourselves at Philip O’Neill Photography with a commitment to quality with each client we work with. Our relaxed, casual approach is our trademark and we thrive on the variety in the projects we tackle. We’re equally comfortable both in studio and on location for a diverse range of clientèle. Our mission is to produce beautiful photographs at affordable prices. Our style is simple, earthy and relaxed.

During a session our main goal is variety whether it is structural or candid, our aim is to capture it all. Capturing the emotion and joy of the moment, we give you a little bit of direction and let the rest fall into place. Making our clients feel at ease, allows us to capture our best work.

Our work is a collaborative effort. Philip Senior is responsible for the large scale direction and vision of Philip O’Neill Photography.

He and daughter Denise personally photograph all of our clients. From the early days with the dark room in his first house, Philip’s evolved in to a digital photography and lighting expert. Philip is an active educator presenting a variety of photography seminars designed for professionals and enthusiasts alike.

Having initially gained his processing expertise in a traditional film laboratory, Philip Junior takes an active hand in nearly every aspect of the company. For the last 10 years he has specialised in high end digital imaging. Philip Junior mainly works behind the scenes, subtly retouching and enhancing the photography to bring out the very best in each project.

Denise joined the family business as a photography assistant and customer focus consultant in 2000 and gained her expertise by assisting Philip on location and in the North Dublin studio. She is involved with all aspects of photography projects from concept to completion, bringing skills in the most current digital imaging technology, as well as a new level of passion for photography. Always classy and intimate her style of imaging stands out on it’s own.

Our studio relocated from Dublin City to Swords in 1993 and is open 10am to 6pm Monday to Saturday, we also open, by appointment, in the evenings. It has a large enough area to accommodate most families which is comfortable and inviting. Many of our consultations, whether they be detailing individual portraits, weddings or commercial projects are held here. We also specialise in children’s fine art portraiture on location or in the comfort of your home. At Philip O’Neill Photography, our staff is preoccupied with producing the finest imagery. Above everything else, we pride ourselves on the care and detailed attention we give each of our clients.
