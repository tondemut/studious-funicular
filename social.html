    Social Photography

We shoot portraits both in our studio in Swords, Dublin, and out and about on location, depending on what our client is looking for. Our team will guide you through the process from start to finish, from what to wear during your session, to advice on how best to present your final artwork. We have a sensitivity for interacting with people, capturing the emotion and personality of each individual. Whether it is out on location, in your home or at our studio in Swords, we will create distinctive images for you to love for years to come.

The best way to prepare for a photo session is not to prepare! Generally children find the experience more fun if it is a spontaneous one, without any pressure and build up to the day. We’re looking for natural expressions and want the experience to be as organic and enjoyable as possible for both parents and children.

We take lots of pictures during your shoot, of which you will select your favourites. Whether you want black & white or colour, studio or location, you will have a wonderful selection to choose from. Picking your pictures can take some time; most people love all the shots and find it very hard to pick their favourites!

Within a week of your session, we will arrange a viewing with you in our studio in Swords. You will be able to select the images you love most and these may then be framed, or presented on canvas or acrylic. Using our creative eye we will suggest different framing and presentation ideas. Whether it’s one large individual photo or several images grouped together as a personality study, our goal is to create artwork that will be included in your family for many generations.

We offer a wide variety of frames and albums, which enables you to display our work to the best effect in your home. Quality is very important to us; our team completes all the framing, matting and design elements in house, at our studio and workshop.

Our session fee at the studio in Swords starts at €55 and increases based on location changes for outdoor shoots. Our framed images are available from €50. We offer canvasses, acrylics and albums.
