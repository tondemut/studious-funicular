    Commercial Photography

Comapny Name Photography is a professional photography supplier catering to many commercial fields; fashion, music/band, live music, portrait, product, advertising and event PR, travel and architectural photography.

Whether you seek one photograph of a small product, or several hundred of products ranging in size, you’ve come to the right place. Our photo studio has the capacity and expertise to shoot products of all kinds and proportions – from jewellery or coffee beans to work wear and furniture. You can check out our Gallery page for some shot of recent commercial jobs.

Fully equipped with the best available professional lighting equipment and spacious shooting areas, our studio is often a preferred location to shoot. Our ample space and range of equipment, coupled with our technical knowledge and efficient workflow, allow our studio to tackle just about any product photography project. Regardless of the scale and specifics of your project, we aim for quality, efficiency, and cost-effectiveness.

When you hire our team to achieve your photographic objectives, we assist you in selecting the style that is most suitable for your product and your market intent. It is our constant objective to provide you with quality photography along with a business experience that is pleasant, effortless and valuable.

Our team is also available to shoot your product on location. The photography that we produce on location is always held to the same high quality standards as our in-studio productions. Whenever and wherever we work, you and your needs are always our number one priority. Our studio equipment is flexible and can be transported to a location of your choice. Our team often scouts the location prior to the day of the shoot to begin the creative and technical process. Our aim is to create imagery that accurately and alluringly displays your subject and location while communicating your idea, mission or message.

Our rates are based on the type of job, how the images will be used, the time involved, and the level of difficulty. Projects are bid on a job by job basis. We bid many of our projects on a flat fee basis to keep the process as simple and predictable as possible for the client. We understand our clients prefer to know a fixed cost in advance.
