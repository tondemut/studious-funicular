    Wedding Photography

Your wedding day is an exciting event, full of busy arrangements, so our photographers presence will be subtle and unobtrusive, capturing snapshots of the day that can be enjoyed years after the occasion.

We appreciate how much care and attention you have put into your day and how essential it is for you to have a stunning collection of images that reflects that. We aim to photograph in detail the candid, natural moments that are all part of your unique story. We shoot weddings in a style that is intimate yet relaxed.

Different genres of photography require a different style and perspective. We would describe our style when shooting weddings as a mix of contemporary photo-journalism combined with classical portraiture. We have a relaxed and natural approach, because often the best shots are taken simply while you’re enjoying your day!

One of the most important things about choosing your wedding photographer is feeling totally comfortable with them, and establishing a good rapport long before the day itself. We like to get a feel for what the bride and groom themselves are looking for, and to get a sense of the couples own vision for their day.

Once a bride and groom has seen our work and decide they’d like to have a chat with us, we arrange an informal wedding consultation in our cosy studio in Swords. This initial meeting gives the couple a chance to browse through our sample albums, and have a bit of a chat with us at the same time.

We also arrange a pre-wedding shoot a few months before the wedding. This is a bit of a practice run for the couple and helps them to feel more familiar and at ease on the wedding day.

A week or two before the wedding itself, we have a final chat about the finer details of the day and any special requests the bride and groom may have. Comfortable that we know the their style inside out, the couple can completely relax and enjoy their day with friends and family, while we capture their story as it unfolds.

After the wedding, we supply the couple with a set of approximately 300 shots to take away and choose from. Once they make their selection we meet again in our studio to design the album together and piece together their unique story.

Your wedding photographs and album are a lifetime investment, one of the few things you have afterwards to remember forever. It’s a privilege for us at Philip O’Neill Photography to play a part in keeping those memories safe.

Our wedding photography packages start from €850 for a mid week wedding and €1250 for weekend weddings. Prices vary depending on the length of coverage required on the day, and the style of album the couple are interested in. We can customize an individual package to suit your budget, so feel free to contact us at our Dublin studio to chat to our photographers about your own requirements.

Some of the features of our wedding packages include:

  • Unlimited photos taken
  • A range of coverage from 4 to 12 hours
  • All photo’s available in colour, black & white and sepia tone
  • A pre-wedding shoot for the couple
  • A pre-wedding &album design meeting
  • A full web gallery complete with personalized ordering system for family and friends
  • High res images supplied on disc ready to print
  • A range of album options from premium leather bound albums to coffee table, magazine style…or both!

Pre-Wedding shoot

A pre-wedding shoot is a great opportunity to take some lovely casual shots of you both. Perfect for those who are a little nervous in front of the camera, it helps you feel even more comfortable with us. A lot of brides say the fun practice run beforehand made them feel completely at ease with the camera on the big day itself. It’s also great for those who want to get some modelling practice in too!

Prints and Enlargements

Prints can be made to order in a range of sizes and finishes. Canvas prints are a fantastic way to present your photos, easy to hang and look fab. We use the highest quality canvas and inks with UV protection, so they’ll still look as good in years to come.

Foam board mounts are another great way to display your photos, and come ready to hang. Other options include acrylic mounts and block mounting, as well as custom framing for any size print.

Parent Albums

Your parents will love a copy of your main album, available in a mini size, as a handy way to carry in their bag to show you off, as all mammys like to do! These great little albums are printed on the highest quality paper and finished with a matt seal to protect the pages, Ask about our discount for multiple orders and mini albums.

Thank You Cards

At your request, we can design and print beautiful personalised thank you cards, in which you can use your favourite shots of the day
